Get involved!

We accept volunteers of almost any magnitude. Volunteers can volunteer for dozens of things.

Volunteers can play with our animals and give them attention and love, pick up supplies (such as animal neccessities from the warehosues where we order our supplies), volunteer their time during adoption events, and plenty more!

All help no matter how big or small is appreciated and helps us a lot!


Volunteer Schedule:

We hold weekly scheduled volunteer events as follows:

Every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday at 8:00 PM we have adoption events where one can volunteer!

Every Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday from 12:00pm-4:00pm we have play hours with our animals, anyone is welmcome!
