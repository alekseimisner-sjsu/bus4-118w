About Us

Who Are We?

Foster Homes for Imaginary Pets (FHFIP) is a non profit anaimal adoption center that aims to find new homes for loving pets. We do not have a parent company or receive funding from organizations such as HSUS or ASPCA. Everybody at FHIP aims to give animals the love they need and to help find them new homes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, protect, and educate all animals and give them the best possible life.

Achievements

  • Saved over 5,000 cats and dogs
  • Reconnect over 200 lost animals with their owners
  • 95% save rate
  • 50 active voluneers
  • Over 300 Spray/Neutuer surgeries
